At AP cleanco, 'remove it' is never the whole story. Every item we collect follows an intentional path — and landfills are genuinely the last resort.

## Donation First

Furniture, appliances, and household goods in working condition are redirected to partnered charities and donation centers. Your old sofa can still serve someone else.

## Recycling Streams

Metals, electronics, and cardboard go to certified recycling facilities. E-waste — old TVs, computers, monitors — is processed in compliance with local regulations to prevent toxic materials from entering soil or water.

## Responsible Disposal

Only what cannot be reused or recycled ends up in disposal. We document the breakdown so clients can receive a disposal report on request.

## Why It Matters

Eco-conscious disposal isn't a marketing tagline. It reflects a straightforward reality: a lot of what fills garages has usable life left — and routing it correctly costs nothing extra to you.
