A garage cleanout is one of the most hands-off home services you can hire — but a small amount of preparation on your end makes a noticeable difference.

## Identify Items in Advance

Walk through the space before the appointment and mentally confirm which items you are parting with. Indecision during the job slows everything down.

## Clear a Path

Crew members move quickly when there is a clear route from your items to the truck. If walls of boxes block the target items, reordering those boxes beforehand saves time.

## Separate Hazardous Materials

Paint, chemicals, propane tanks, and batteries require special handling. Set these aside and flag them for the crew at check-in — do not mix them into the general removal pile without flagging first.

## Be Present (or Designate Someone)

We require authorization before removing any item. Being on-site — or having a trusted person present — ensures nothing gets removed that shouldn't, and the job proceeds without unneeded pauses.
