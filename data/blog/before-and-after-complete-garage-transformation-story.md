When a client in San Jose called AP Cleanco, they estimated they had "maybe a dozen boxes and some old furniture." What we found on arrival was quite different — a two-car garage packed floor-to-ceiling after fifteen years of accumulation.

## The Assessment

Our team walked the space, categorized items visually, and gave the homeowner clear options: full removal, selective removal, or a hybrid approach with donation routing for good-condition items.

## Day of Service

Two crew members worked the space efficiently — heavy items first (a riding lawnmower, two broken refrigerators, a treadmill), followed by bulk furniture, and finally the miscellaneous boxes.

## The Result

By early afternoon, the floor was bare and swept. The homeowner described it as "unrecognizable — in the best way."

The three-car space now has room for storage shelving, a workbench, and — for the first time in over a decade — both cars park inside.
