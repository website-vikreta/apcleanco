We all let clutter build up — but at some point, a garage crosses a threshold where professional help isn't just convenient, it's necessary. Here are five unmistakable signs.

## 1. You Can't Access Emergency Equipment

If your fire extinguisher, first aid kit, or emergency exit is blocked, the space has become a safety hazard.

## 2. You can't Park Your Car

A garage that can no longer accommodate its primary purpose is overdue for a serious reset.

## 3. There Are Signs of Pests

Clutter creates nesting grounds. If you spot chewed boxes or droppings, a thorough cleanout is step one before any pest treatment.

## 4. Items Haven't Been Touched in Years

Items untouched for two or more years are almost certainly no longer needed — and are simply occupying valuable real estate.

## 5. The Task Feels Paralyzing

When the sheer volume of stuff stops you from starting, professional help removes both the labor and the decision fatigue.
